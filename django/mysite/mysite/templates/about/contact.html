My Story:

I am Timo and I love to take on new and exciting projects. When I was little I have always liked sports and for a long time I was determined to become a professional golfer. This has taught me a lot about life. At some point I realized that in golf I was not getting anymore experience, but simply doing more of the same. Around this time I also started my undergraduate program in mechanical engineering at the Delft University of Technology. This is where I realized that even if you make it as a professional golfer this does not nessecarily mean you are a succesfull and nice person. There are so many other things you can do!

Since I have started my studies I have tried to take on many projects to get more experience and try to make my own story. For example I took on two student jobs in a related field to my study. Two years ago I started at Prime Vision to do CAD design and 3D printing. I designed concepts for there autonomous robots. It was a lot of fun and it also helped me realized that I needed a lot more knowledge in programming and PCB design that I was not going to get in my mechanical engineering program. After this job I took on another job in the process engineering industry at Psicon where I still work mostly I do CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics) analysis in ansys. But in the future I will also do process automation projects.

As I have lived in the Rotterdam region all my live I decided it would be a great experience to live somewhere else for a while. I applied for an exchange to Imperial College London which is a very prestiguous exchange. (you need a 7.5 average). I got the spot and now I live in central London studying both Electrical Engineering and Computer science in one of the best university in the world. It definitely is as cool as it sounds!
